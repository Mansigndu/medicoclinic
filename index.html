Our Specialized Services

Comprehensive care for various medical conditions. Accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and long-term management with a patient-first approach.

Rheumatoid Arthritis

Comprehensive care for Rheumatoid Arthritis, focusing on reducing joint pain, inflammation, and stiffness. We provide early diagnosis and personalized treatment plans to improve mobility and quality of life.

Different Types of Arthritis

Diagnosis and treatment of various types of arthritis including osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and gout. We focus on reducing pain, improving joint function, and enhancing overall mobility.

Lupus (SLE)

Expert care for Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE), an autoimmune condition affecting multiple organs. Focused on early diagnosis, symptom control, and long-term management to improve quality of life.

Scleroderma

Specialized care for Scleroderma, a chronic autoimmune condition affecting skin and internal organs. Focused on early diagnosis, symptom management, and improving overall quality of life.

Vasculitis

Diagnosis and treatment of Vasculitis, a condition involving inflammation of blood vessels. Focused on controlling symptoms, preventing complications, and improving overall vascular health.

Osteoporosis

Comprehensive care for Osteoporosis, focusing on bone health, fracture prevention, and improving bone density. Personalized treatment and lifestyle guidance for long-term strength and mobility.

About Medico Clinic

Medico Clinic is a trusted center for specialized rheumatology care, focusing on accurate diagnosis and management of complex joint, autoimmune, and inflammatory conditions. Using modern diagnostics and evidence-based treatments, we prioritize patient well-being and long-term health outcomes.

Led by Dr. Shailaja Sabnis, with 30+ years of experience, our clinic delivers personalized care to improve quality of life and ensure sustainable health results.
